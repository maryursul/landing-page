General Info

About us

"Little Mary beauty"- is a full service women’s hair salon located in Lviv. Our professional team is current with all the latest styles and trends to provide you with your best look. We specialize in Deva cuts, single-process color, highlighting, and Pintura. We strive for the highest quality of service, while maintaining a fun, friendly, non-pretentious environment.
